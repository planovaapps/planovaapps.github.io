Privacy Policy

Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how QuickPlan ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in our data practices.

Information We Collect
We take a privacy-first approach and do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information ("PII") from your device. All your data, including but not limited to tasks, notes, schedules, and preferences, remains stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Third-Party Services
Our application may contain third-party services such as advertising networks or analytics providers. These third parties may collect certain technical information including device identifiers, usage patterns, and app performance metrics in accordance with their respective privacy policies. We do not control the data collection practices of these third parties and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

How We Use Your Information
Since we do not collect personal data, we cannot and do not use your personal information for any purpose. Any data you create within the application remains under your exclusive control and is processed locally on your device for the sole purpose of providing the intended functionality of the Service.

Information Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. As we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to share. We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be used to identify you for the purpose of improving our services.

Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data. However, as your data remains locally stored on your device, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and any backup systems you may use.
