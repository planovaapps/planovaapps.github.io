Request to Delete Your Data

QuickPlan does not collect or store any personal data on our servers. All your data (tasks, reminders, settings) is stored locally on your device.

How to Delete Your Data

  • Option 1: Delete your tasks manually within the QuickPlan app.
  • Option 2: Uninstall the app — this will remove all locally stored data.
  • Option 3: Contact us at Support QuickPlan if you need help with data removal.

What Data Is Deleted

All task-related data, reminders, preferences, and theme settings will be erased when the app is uninstalled. We do not back up or sync any user data externally.

Retention Period

No data is retained once the app is removed. Data is not synced or stored by our servers.
